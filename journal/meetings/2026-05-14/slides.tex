\begin{frame}{Overview}
\begin{itemize}\setlength{\itemsep}{0.45em}
\item \textbf{Instrument} An updated version of the instrument was implemented (muni-relative owner-share, pre-earliest-election window).
\item \textbf{Two taxonomies.} Aggregate BNDES industries (Agriculture, Industry, Infrastructure, Services); firm-size (Small $\leq$ 49, Medium 50--249, Big $\geq$ 250).
\item \textbf{Four control specs.} (1) No controls; (2) $+$ EC (per-cell exposure control); (3) $+$ Vol (volume control); (4) $+$ Vol $+$ EC.
\item Volume control: total BNDES disbursements over 2002 municipal GDP.
\item \textbf{Four alignment channels.} Mayor; Mayor $\cdot$ President; Mayor $\cdot$ Governor; Mayor $\cdot$ Gov.\ $\cdot$ President.
\item \textbf{Sample.} 5,544 municipalities; 88,694 municipality-years.
\item All regressions use municipality and year fixed effects; standard errors clustered by municipality.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Summary of Results}
\begin{itemize}\setlength{\itemsep}{0.45em}
\item The AR test \textit{rejects} at 5\% in some specs for both BNDES sectors and firm size.
\item The volume control shifts $F$ negligibly and does not have a significant nor relevant coefficient; the EC controls (per-cell) move $F$ in both directions.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
