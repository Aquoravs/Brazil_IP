\section{The three regressions on the slides}\label{sec:eq}

Every cell on the slides comes from a cluster-robust regression at the
municipality--year level, with municipality and year fixed effects
$\alpha_m,\delta_t$ and standard errors clustered by municipality. The deck
reports three kinds of regression, each run at two margins: the four BNDES
aggregate sectors, and BNDES sector $\times$ firm size (12 groups).

\paragraph{(a) First stage of the sector shares (slides 4--5).}
The endogenous object is the vector $s_{mt}$ of sector employment shares. For an
instrument stack $\cS$ (a set of channels, \autoref{sec:stacks}), each share is
projected on the stack's full instrument block:
\begin{equation}\label{eq:fs-shares}
  s_{jmt}
  \;=\;
  \alpha^{(j)}_m + \delta^{(j)}_t
  \;+\; \Pi_j'\,Z^{\cS}_{mt}
  \;+\; \Phi_j'\,\EC^{\cS}_{mt}
  \;+\; \mu_j\,\Vol_{mt}
  \;+\; \nu_{jmt}.
\end{equation}
This is the first stage embedded in the AR-test IV system; $\EC$ and $\Vol$
are included controls. Reported: the Sanderson--Windmeijer $F$ for each share
and the Kleibergen--Paap rank statistic of the block (\autoref{sec:diag}).

\paragraph{(b) First stage of the volume control (slide 6).}
The volume ratio is regressed, one channel $c$ at a time, on that channel's
muni-aggregated instrument $\Zbar^{c}_{mt}=\sum_j Z^{c}_{jmt}$:
\begin{equation}\label{eq:fs-vol}
  \Vol_{mt}
  \;=\;
  \alpha_m + \delta_t
  \;+\; \pi_c\,\Zbar^{c}_{mt}
  \;+\; \Phi_c'\,\EC^{c}_{mt}
  \;+\; \nu_{mt}.
\end{equation}
Reported: the cluster-robust partial $F$ on $\Zbar^{c}$. Channels clearing the
5\% gate are the volume instruments used in treatment~(iii) below.

\paragraph{(c) The AR test --- baseline screen (slides 7--8).}
The AR reduced form, run once per excluded stack $\cS$:
\begin{equation}\label{eq:rf}
  \log\mathrm{GDP}_{mt}
  \;=\;
  \alpha_m + \delta_t
  \;+\; \gamma'\,Z^{\cS}_{mt}
  \;+\; \psi'\,\EC^{\cS}_{mt}
  \;+\; \tog{(\text{volume treatment})}
  \;+\; \eta_{mt}.
\end{equation}
This is the GDP equation with $s_{mt}$ substituted out through its first
stage~\eqref{eq:fs-shares}. The reported \textbf{AR joint $F$} is the
cluster-robust Wald statistic for $\gamma=\bzero$ --- the joint significance of
the stack's instruments \emph{only}; $\EC^{\cS}$ and the volume term are
partialled out, not tested.

\section{The seven channels and the excluded-stack design}\label{sec:stacks}

Each alignment channel $c$ pairs a set of political offices with an alignment
shock $\Align^{c}_{mpt}$; the cross-office shocks are products of the
tier-specific alignment indicators.

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{lll}
\toprule
Channel & Offices & $\Align^{c}_{mpt}=1$ when party $p$\ldots \\
\midrule
M               & mayor                & holds the mayoralty of $m$ \\
G               & governor             & is in the governor's coalition of $s(m)$ \\
P               & president            & is in the presidential coalition \\
M$\cdot$P       & mayor, pres.         & holds the mayoralty \emph{and} is in the presidential coalition \\
M$\cdot$G       & mayor, gov.          & holds the mayoralty \emph{and} is in the governor's coalition \\
G$\cdot$P       & gov., pres.          & is in the governor's \emph{and} the presidential coalition \\
M$\cdot$G$\cdot$P & mayor, gov., pres. & is aligned at all three tiers simultaneously \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

A \textbf{stack} $\cS$ is a set of channels. Equations~\eqref{eq:fs-shares}
and~\eqref{eq:rf} enter the full per-sector instrument block $Z^{c}$ of
\emph{every} channel in $\cS$ as excluded instruments: $Z^{\cS}_{mt}$ stacks
those blocks, $\EC^{\cS}_{mt}$ the matching exposure controls. Thus
$\{$M,\,P,\,M$\cdot$P$\}$ means $Z^{\mathrm M}$, $Z^{\mathrm P}$, \emph{and}
$Z^{\mathrm{M\cdot P}}$ are all excluded instruments at once. Eighteen stacks
are evaluated:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*,nosep]
\item \textbf{Singletons} (7): each channel alone.
\item \textbf{Mayor stacks} (4): $\{$M,\,M$\cdot$P$\}$, $\{$M,\,M$\cdot$G$\}$,
  $\{$M,\,M$\cdot$G$\cdot$P$\}$, $\{$M,\,M$\cdot$P,\,M$\cdot$G,\,M$\cdot$G$\cdot$P$\}$.
\item \textbf{Parent pairs} (3): $\{$M,\,G$\}$, $\{$M,\,P$\}$, $\{$G,\,P$\}$.
\item \textbf{Parent $+$ interaction} (4): $\{$M,\,P,\,M$\cdot$P$\}$,
  $\{$M,\,G,\,M$\cdot$G$\}$, $\{$G,\,P,\,G$\cdot$P$\}$,
  $\{$M,\,G,\,P,\,M$\cdot$G$\cdot$P$\}$.
\end{itemize}

\section{The three volume treatments}\label{sec:vol}

The volume term in~\eqref{eq:rf} is toggled across three treatments.

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{lll}
\toprule
Slide label & Volume term in~\eqref{eq:rf} & Role \\
\midrule
No vol.\          & ---                                  & volume left uncontrolled \\
Vol.\ control     & $\tog{\lambda\,\Vol_{mt}}$, included  & volume as a predetermined control \\
Vol.\ instr.\     & $\tog{\lambda\,\Vol_{mt}}$, instrumented & Full IV: $\Vol_{mt}$ instrumented \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

Under \emph{Vol.\ instr.}, $\Vol_{mt}$ is instrumented by $\Zbar^{c}$ of the
volume-relevant channels from~\eqref{eq:fs-vol} that are \emph{not} in the
stack $\cS$ --- using a channel already in $\cS$ would make $\Zbar^{c}$
collinear with that stack's excluded $Z^{c}$ block.

\section{Diagnostics}\label{sec:diag}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*,nosep]
\item \textbf{Sanderson--Windmeijer $F$} --- the cluster-robust first-stage $F$
  for one endogenous share, conditional on the other shares. ``Identified
  shares'' counts shares with SW $F\ge10$.
\item \textbf{Kleibergen--Paap rank statistic} --- the cluster-robust rank test
  for the stack's instrument block, the relevance diagnostic for a
  vector-valued endogenous object.
\item \textbf{AR joint $F$} --- the cluster-robust Wald test of $\gamma=\bzero$
  in~\eqref{eq:rf}, computed on the stack's instrument coefficients only.
\end{itemize}

\section{Variable construction}\label{sec:vars}

\paragraph{Outcome --- $\log\mathrm{GDP}_{mt}$.}
Municipal log GDP (2018 prices), 2002--2017.

\paragraph{Endogenous shares --- $s_{jmt}$.}
Sector $j$'s share of municipality $m$'s formal employment in year $t$, from
RAIS. The simplex hold-out sector is the omitted base, leaving $J-1$ endogenous
shares.

\paragraph{Instrument --- $Z^{c}_{jmt}$ (muni-relative owner share).}
$Z^{c}_{jmt}=\sum_{p}\tilde w^{c}_{jmp,t}\,\Align^{c}_{mpt}$, one column per
retained sector. The weight
\[
  \tilde w^{c}_{jmp,t}
  \;=\;
  \frac{\sum_{s\in T^{c}_{t}}\sum_{f\in\cF(j,m)} L_{f,p,s}}
       {\sum_{j'}\sum_{p'}\sum_{s\in T^{c}_{t}}\sum_{f\in\cF(j',m)} L_{f,p',s}}
\]
is cell $(j,m)$'s share of municipality $m$'s pre-window affiliated owner-years
at party $p$; $L_{f,p,s}$ counts owners of firm $f$ affiliated with party $p$ in
year $s$ (unaffiliated owners dropped), and $\cF(j,m)$ is the set of firms ever
active in sector $j$ of $m$. The window
$T^{c}_{t}=[\,e^{\min}_{c}(t)-4,\;e^{\min}_{c}(t)-1\,]\cap[2002,2017]$ closes
before any office in channel $c$ can turn over, with $e^{\min}_{c}(t)$ the most
recent election --- among the channel's offices --- on or before $t$.

\paragraph{Exposure control --- $\EC^{c}_{jm,t}$.}
The same weights summed over parties without the shift,
$\EC^{c}_{jm,t}=\sum_{p}\tilde w^{c}_{jmp,t}$: cell $(j,m)$'s share of the
municipality's affiliated owner mass. It absorbs the share-component
confounding of the shift-share instrument; one column per retained sector per
channel.

\paragraph{Aggregate channel instrument --- $\Zbar^{c}_{mt}$.}
$\Zbar^{c}_{mt}=\sum_j Z^{c}_{jmt}$, the channel's instrument summed over
sectors. It is the scalar instrument for $\Vol_{mt}$ in the volume first
stage~\eqref{eq:fs-vol} and in the Full-IV treatment.

\paragraph{Volume control --- $\Vol_{mt}$.}
$\Vol_{mt}=\mathrm{BNDES\_total}_{mt}/\mathrm{GDP}_{m,2002}$: current-year total
municipal BNDES disbursements over initial-period (2002) municipal GDP. The
denominator is fixed pre-treatment, so $\Vol_{mt}$ varies in $t$ only through
the numerator.
